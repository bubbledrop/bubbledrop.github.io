Interesting message types

  • ADT - admission/discharge/transfer of patients in different departments. There are 51 types of ADT messages, and they are amongst the most commonly seen inside hospitals.
  • ORM and ORU - messages for observation requests "take a measurement" and observation results (which may be spontaneous or triggered by request. Specifically the OBX segments of ORU^R01 messages contain data about the measurement itself which can often be used to recognize the type of device.
  • DFT - financial transactions. SIU - scheduling information. ACK - should be returned after every message, contains only an MSH segment.
