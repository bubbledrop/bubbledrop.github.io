What is HL7

  • HL7 (Health Level 7) is a set of standards produced by the HL7 International, an international standards organization based in Ann Arbor, Michigan and subsequently adopted by other standard organizations such as ISO and ANSI.
  • The primary focus is on communication protocols in the application layer (layer 7) of the OSI model.
  • These protocols are specifically designed for the transfer of clinical and administrative data between devices of various healthcare device vendors and service providers.
