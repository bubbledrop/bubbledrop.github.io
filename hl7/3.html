v2.x HL7 protocol - Introduction

  • Existing versions are 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.3.1, 2.4, 2.5, 2.5.1, 2.6, 2.7, 2.7.1, 2.8, 2.8.1, 2.8.2.
  • Application layer, implementations over TCP/UDP or RS232 (serial).
  • For TCP/UDP, optional encapsulation in MLLP for messages that exceed the MTU (more on this later).
  • Textual with certain ASCII characters serving as delimiters between information elements.
    • Therefore cannot support most unicode charsets, but UTF-8 is fine.
    • The delimiters are specified in the header of every message.
    • But there is also de-facto standard for their values.
