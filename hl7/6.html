The MSH segment

MSH|^~\&|GHH LAB|ELAB-3|GHH OE|BLDG4|200202150930||ORU^R01|CNTRL-3456|P|2.4<CR>
  • Other important information which may be obtained from the MSH segment:
    • MSH-3 (sending application), MSH-4 (sending facility), MSH-5 (receiving application), MSH-6 (receiving facility). May be informative when appropriately configured.
    • MSH-11 specifies the HL7 version and is important for interoperability across different versions.
  • MSH is the only segment that is mandatory in all HL7 messages.
  • As explained before, the MSH-9 field (here "ORU^R01") declares the message type and must be read to properly parse all following segments. There are over 100 different HL7 message types, categorized by type (the first three letters, here ORU stands for "observation result") and further subindexed (here R01).
